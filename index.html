Luxury Radar

We are building brand identifier app, which requires a picture from users and return the brands of the clothes. It can also tell the users where they can buy the clothes in the picture by providing them the web stores link lists and the locations of the stores on the map. The reason we are building this app is that we noticed that a lot of people are interested at what celebrities or others are wearing and they want to know how to get the same style clothes. By using this app, they will have an easy way to follow the fashion trend and know how to get the clothes they like.
